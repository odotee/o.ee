We’ve successfully upgraded [**P.LU**](https://p.lu/) to **PeerTube 7.2.2**! This release brings important **security improvements**, **bug fixes**, and **better accessibility**.

🔗 For full technical details, see the [official release notes](https://github.com/Chocobozzz/PeerTube/releases/tag/v7.2.2)

---

## 🔐 Highlights

- Fixed a security issue affecting user tracking
- Improved video uploading and playback stability
- Enhanced accessibility across the platform

---

## 📝 Please Note

**Account registration on P.LU requires manual approval.**  

To get approved, please explain clearly why you’re signing up. Incomplete or unclear requests will be declined.
